Write the three forms of Ohm's Law ($E = \cdots$ \hskip 10pt , $I = \cdots$ \hskip 10pt and $R = \cdots$), showing how voltage, current, and resistance relate to one another:

\underbar{file 03336}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

$E = IR$ \hskip 30pt $I = {E \over R}$ \hskip 30pt $R = {E \over I}$
