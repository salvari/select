What is the purpose of an {\it oscillator} circuit?  What function does it perform?

\underbar{file 02605}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

An oscillator creates an AC or pulsed DC waveform from steady DC power.

%(END_ANSWER)
