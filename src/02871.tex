Define what a "high" logic signal is in a digital logic gate circuit.

\underbar{file 02871}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

A voltage signal (measured with respect to ground) near the positive supply voltage rail.

%(END_ANSWER)
