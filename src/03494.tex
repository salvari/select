Calculate the reluctance ($\Re$) for a magnetic circuit where the MMF ($\cal F$) is 8.9 amp-turns and the flux ($\Phi$) is 0.24 webers.

\underbar{file 03494}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

$\Re$ = 37.08 amp-turns per weber (At/Wb)

%(END_ANSWER)
