Define {\it electromagnetic induction}, in your own words.

\underbar{file 03478}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

{\it Electromagnetic induction} is the production of a voltage along a conductor by a magnetic field whose flux changes over time.

%(END_ANSWER)
