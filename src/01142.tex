What is a {\it VCO}?

\underbar{file 01142}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

A {\it VCO} is a Voltage Controlled Oscillator.

%(END_ANSWER)

Ask your students how a VCO might be used in a circuit.  What, exactly, does "voltage controlled" mean?
