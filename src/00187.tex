A 10 $\mu$F capacitor is charged to a voltage of 20 volts.  How many coulombs of electric charge are stored in this capacitor?

\underbar{file 00187}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

200 $\mu$C of charge.
