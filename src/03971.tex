What types of transistors are used inside "TTL" style logic gate integrated circuits, as opposed to "CMOS" logic gate integrated circuits?

\underbar{file 03971}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

TTL gate circuits are made from bipolar (PNP/NPN) transistors.

%(END_ANSWER)
