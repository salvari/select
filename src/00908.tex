What effect does doping concentration have on the electrical conductivity of an extrinsic semiconductor?

\underbar{file 00908}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

The more concentrated the "doping," the greater the conductivity of the material.

%(END_ANSWER)
