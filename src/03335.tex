%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

{\bf Voltage}: Electrical "pressure," or motivating force.

\vskip 10pt

{\bf Current}: Flow of electrical charges (motion).

\vskip 10pt

{\bf Resistance}: Opposition (friction) to the flow of charges (current).
