Explain why some oscillator circuits make use of {\it crystals}.

\underbar{file 02629}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

Crystals act as high-Q resonant elements, stabilizing the frequency of an oscillator better than most LC "tank circuits" could.

%(END_ANSWER)
