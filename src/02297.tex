What must be done to an intrinsic semiconductor to turn it into an "N-type" semiconductor?

\underbar{file 02297}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

A pentavalent dopant must be added to it, to create donor electrons.

%(END_ANSWER)
