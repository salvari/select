Calculate the amount of MMF ($\cal F$) generated by a coil of wire having 1300 turns and carrying 3.5 milliamperes of current.

\underbar{file 03491}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

$\cal F$ = 4.55 amp-turns

%(END_ANSWER)
