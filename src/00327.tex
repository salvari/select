What is the difference between a {\it fuse} and a {\it circuit breaker}?

\underbar{file 00327}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

A fuse is for one-time use, while a circuit breaker may be re-set and used repeatedly.

%(END_ANSWER)
