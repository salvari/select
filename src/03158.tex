{\it Multiplexing} is a very important concept in long distance telephone systems.  Briefly explain \underbar{why} multiple telephone conversations are "multiplexed" over long-distance lines.

\underbar{file 03158}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

Individual telephone calls are multiplexed so that many calls may be transmitted over a single pair of wires (or single optical fiber).
