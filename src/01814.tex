Calculate the amount of time it takes for a 33 $\mu$F capacitor to charge from 0 volts to 20 volts, if powered by a 24 volt battery through a 10 k$\Omega$ resistor.

\underbar{file 01814}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

0.591 seconds

%(END_ANSWER)
