What is the typical forward voltage drop for a light-emitting diode?  What is the typical forward current for an LED?

\underbar{file 01027}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

LED forward current is 20 mA.  Forward voltage varies with color.

%(END_ANSWER)
