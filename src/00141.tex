How many atoms of iron are there in a sample measuring $2 \times 10^3$ moles?  Determine the answer without using a calculator!

\underbar{file 00141}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

There are $1.2044 \times 10^{27}$ atoms of iron in this sample.

%(END_ANSWER)
