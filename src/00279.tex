What is a {\it busbar}?

\underbar{file 00279}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

A "busbar" is a conductor of unusually large cross-sectional area.

%(END_ANSWER)





%(BEGIN_NOTES)

Ask your students to describe possible applications for busbars.  Where would they typically encounter such huge conductors of electricity?
