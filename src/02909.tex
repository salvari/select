Identify the type of logic gate that performs the Boolean function of {\it inversion} $(\overline{A})$.

\underbar{file 02909}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

The inverter, or NOT gate, performs Boolean inversion.

%(END_ANSWER)
