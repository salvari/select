Explain what it means for an inductor-capacitor ($LC$) circuit to be in a state of {\it resonance}.

\vskip 30pt

\underbar{file 03616}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

Resonance is that state where the inductive and capacitive reactances are precisely equal to one another:

$$X_L = X_C$$

%(END_ANSWER)
