In a parallel circuit, which variable or variables ($V$, $I$, or $P$) add to equal the total?

\underbar{file 03398}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

Both {\it current} and {\it power} add to equal the total in a parallel circuit.
