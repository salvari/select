What sort of immediate medical attention does a victim of severe electrical shock require?  Explain why this attention is necessary.

\underbar{file 00300}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

Cardio-pulmonary resuscitation (CPR).

%(END_ANSWER)
