$$6 + 2(5^3 - 100) = $$

%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

$$6 + 2(5^3 - 100) = 56$$

$$\left({{55 - 6} \over 7}\right)^2 - \log 100 = 47$$

$$3 \sqrt{6^2 - 11} = 15$$
