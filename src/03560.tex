Describe the difference between {\it DC} and {\it AC} electricity, in your own words.

\underbar{file 03560}
%(END_QUESTION)





%(BEGIN_ANSWER)

DC moves in the same direction (has the same polarity), while AC switches direction (polarity) back and forth over time.

%(END_ANSWER)
